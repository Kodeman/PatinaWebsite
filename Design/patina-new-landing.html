The craftspeople

Fifty families of makers. Generations of craft.

Every piece in our collection comes from makers we trust—artisans whose work has been refined over decades, sometimes centuries. These are their stories.

Slow Made

Nordic Atelier

Aarhus, Denmark

Est. 1968 · 56 years

Three generations of woodcraft. Their joinery technique—learned from grandfather to father to son—creates furniture that tightens with age, not loosens.

4th Generation

Woodward & Sons

Richmond, Virginia

Est. 1889 · 135 years

Hand-turned in Virginia since before electric power reached the workshop. Today, the great-great-grandson still uses the original lathe—because it makes better legs.

Master Craftsman

Sashimono Studio

Kyoto, Japan

Est. 1923 · 101 years

Sashimono joinery uses no nails, no screws—just wood fitted to wood so precisely it needs nothing else. Each piece takes months. The wait is part of the value.

The experience

How it feels

Not a product tour. A way of discovering what belongs in your home.

First, you walk your room with us

Point your phone and move through the space. While you scan, gentle questions emerge—not a quiz, just a conversation about what you're looking for.

By the time your room is mapped, we already understand more than any questionnaire could capture.

Then, you see ten pieces—not ten thousand

Every recommendation is vetted by professional designers. Not just "customers also bought"—but "this leather ages beautifully" and "watch the scale if your ceilings are under nine feet."

"I was skeptical about an app understanding my style. But the recommendations were eerily accurate—it showed me pieces I didn't know I wanted but immediately loved."
— Alex R., Denver

Watch the morning light catch the grain

See the piece in your actual room. Not a floating 3D model—placed where it would live, casting real shadows, catching your light.

Slide through sunrise to sunset. Watch how leather warms in afternoon sun. Know it works before it arrives.

"The AR feature saved me from a huge mistake. I could see the sofa was too large before ordering."
— Sarah M., Chicago

Finally, you bring it home knowing it belongs

Every piece ships from makers we trust, with the story of who made it and how to care for it. No surprises—just furniture that was meant for your space.

And if you ever want to go deeper, your profile transfers seamlessly to a professional designer. No starting over. No re-explaining.

Designer-taught recommendations

Interior designers have spent decades learning what makes a room feel right. We asked them to teach what they know—and built something that remembers.

Every piece in our catalog is analyzed by professional designers who add the insights you won't find on a product page: "This leather only gets better with Sunday afternoons." "Marble needs coasters—always." "Perfect for pet-free homes."

The more you explore, the better the recommendations become. Not because we're tracking you—because we're learning your language. Some preferences are hard to put into words. Maybe you linger on weathered oak but scroll past polished chrome. Maybe warm tones make you pause. We pay attention so you don't have to explain.
